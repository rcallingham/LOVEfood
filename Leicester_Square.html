NOPI

Middle Eastern and Asian-inspired Sharing

Along with the more well-known Ottolenghi cafes NOPI is owed by award winning chef Yotam Ottolenghi. With a similar feel to Ottolenghi this more formal restaurant still has strong Middle Eastern influences with bold flavours from the Mediterranean and Asia. You can go the conventional route, with starters and mains, or take the opportunity for wider grazing by sticking to sharing plates (but these are quite small). Vegetarians have plenty of choice.Not the lightest on the purse-strings but the sophisticated atmosphere and delightful food will not leave you disappointed.
